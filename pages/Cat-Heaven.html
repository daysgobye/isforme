Fun Cat Facts

  • Cats spend about 70% of their lives sleeping!
  • A group of cats is called a "clowder".
  • Cats have 32 muscles in each ear, allowing them to move their ears independently.
  • A cat's purr vibrates at a frequency of 25 to 150 hertz, which can promote healing.
  • Cats can jump up to 6 times their length.

Cat Care Tips

Taking care of our feline friends is important! Here are some basic tips:

  • Feed your cat a balanced diet appropriate for their age and health.
  • Provide fresh water daily.
  • Regular vet check-ups are essential.
  • Keep the litter box clean.
  • Provide plenty of play and exercise opportunities.
  • Show them lots of love and affection!
